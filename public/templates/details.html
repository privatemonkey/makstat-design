Statistical release Published: 2022-11-11, 11:00

Tourism, September 2022

The total number of tourists in September 2022 was 103 787, an increase of 89.89% from September 2021.

The number of tourists in September 2022 was 103 787, of which 32.9% were domestic tourists and the other 67.1% were foreign tourists.

The number of nights spent in September 2022 was 221 927, of which 36.9% are realised by domestic and the other 63.1% are realised by foreign tourists.

In the period January - September 2022, compared to the same period last year, the number of tourists increased by 37.0%: the number of domestic tourists increased by 4.3%, while that of foreign tourists increased by 88.2%.

In the period January - September 2022, compared to the same period last year, the number of nights spent increased by 21.3%: the number of nights spent by domestic tourists increased by 2.4%, while those by foreign tourists increased by 76.2%.
